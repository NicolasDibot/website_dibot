Applied AI innovation

Applied machine learning / AI services on complex data: imaging, audio, and tabular data, with a focus on biomedical data.

Audit and needs assessment

Meetings with your teams, available data, goal definition.

Proposal and quote

Estimated duration or fixed price, exact cost, number of iterations, payment terms.

Project delivery

Iterations, validated deliverables, documentation and handover.

Examples

  • Automated medical imaging triage to prioritize urgent cases.
  • Detection of incomplete records in a clinical information system using supervised models on tabular data.
  • Robustness and bias audit of a predictive model already used internally.
  • LLM training for healthcare managers: useful use cases, prompt design, limitations, and GDPR.
  • Automatic detection of aberrant images (bad framing, artifacts, noisy data).
  • Automated extraction of clinical variables from structured or semi-structured PDFs.
